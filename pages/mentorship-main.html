Alumni Mentorship

Why do we need this program?

Students after coming to DTU are overwhelmed by the countless opportunities present here and often end up confused or going with the herd. Through this program, we aspire to give the students access to our diverse global alumni network to gain new perspectives and responsibly plan their career trajectories.

How does this program work?

1

Present DTU students and DTU alumni need to fill our Exhaustive Registration Form

2

Algorithm driven mapping connects the students and mentors based on their interests and goals.

3

Enrolled students get access to mentoring sessions and one on one guidance

Why choose this program?

For Alumni

  • Gain fulfillment by helping students
  • Expand expertise
  • Improve communication and listening
  • Create legacy
  • Stay up-to-date with the next generation of pioneers

For Students

  • Career Guidance
  • Thorough knowledge of topics
  • Interact with similar mindsets
  • Explore more career options
  • Network with industry experts

Frequently Asked Questions

Mentees: Currently enrolled students of DTU

Mentors: DTU alumni having 4+ years of industry experience who are willing to dedicate atleast 2 hours per month on mentoring students

The program is free and exclusive to DTU students

Students are connected to the most relevant mentors based on thier goals and interests expressed in the registration form.
