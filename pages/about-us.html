About Us

Welcome to the Alumni Affairs Portal of Delhi Technological University

Our key objective is to nurture and strengthen the Institute's bond with its alumni community, business corporations and philanthropists. These groups are the key stakeholders in DTU's journey of excellence. We encourage the involvement and participation of these entities in the Institute's strategic initiatives. These include setting up world class research and academics in emerging areas of science and technology, building world class infrastructure, improving faculty value proposition, strengthening the entrepreneurship ecosystem, and contributing to the society.

Our Vision

DTU aspires to forge a lifelong strong relationship with it's alumni and create opportunities to strengthen bonds between them and their alma mater.

Our Mission

One-to-one interaction with alumni and strong bonding between Alumni and DTU. Coordination between illustrious alumni and current students for job and training prospects. Creation of a comprehensive Alumni database. Sponsorship and fund raising for strategic planning and infrastructure development.

Quality Policy

The University is committed to achieving global standards of excellence in the field of Science, Technology, Management and allied areas by disseminating knowledge through cutting-edge research, education and innovation. We adopt best practices to maintain high standards in the core and allied functions through continuous evaluation and improvement of our processes.

Core Values

Integrity

We do what we say through trust, transparency and honesty.

Compassion

We foster civilized mindset for kindness, consideration and benevolence.

Commitment

We dedicate ourselves completely to all our endeavors.

Creativity

We nurture innovation, imagination and ideation.

Collaboration

We promote team work through togetherness, harmony and acknowledgment.

Inclusion

We embrace diversity with respect, acceptance and affirmation.

Dean's Message

Dear Alumni,
I am proud to acknowledge the vital role our alumni play in DTU's growth and success. Your achievements reflect the values we foster, and we are committed to deepening the bond between you and the university.

Our mission is to build a vibrant ecosystem where alumni, students, and faculty collaborate, sharing knowledge and opportunities. Through mentorship, industry partnerships, and networking events, we aim to create meaningful connections that drive personal and professional growth.

Your involvement in new initiatives—whether entrepreneurship, research, or community service—can shape DTU's future. Alumni-led efforts in start-up incubators, sustainability, and innovation reflect the spirit that defines us.

We welcome your support through volunteer roles, mentorship, or financial contributions to scholarships, research and infrastructure development. The details of possible initiatives is available on the alumni portal. Together, we can nurture future leaders and keep DTU at the forefront of global developments.

Your connection to DTU is lifelong. Let's continue this journey of excellence and create lasting legacies that inspire generations to come.
Warm regards,
Prof Vishal Verma
Dean, Alumni Affairs
